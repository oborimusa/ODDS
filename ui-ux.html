FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between UI and UX?

UX (User Experience) is about how a product works and feels — the overall experience. UI (User Interface) is about how it looks — the visual design. Both are essential, and we provide both.

How long does the design process take?

A complete UI/UX design for a website typically takes 3-4 weeks. Mobile apps take 4-6 weeks. Complex products may take longer. We'll provide a timeline during discovery.

How much does UI/UX design cost?

Pricing depends on complexity. Website UI/UX packages start from ₦350,000, mobile apps from ₦500,000. Contact us for a custom quote based on your needs.

Do you design for web, mobile, or both?

Both! We design responsive websites that work on all devices, as well as native mobile apps for iOS and Android.

Do you conduct user testing?

Absolutely. We believe in designing with users, not just for them. We conduct usability testing at key stages and iterate based on feedback.

What files will I receive?

You'll receive design files (Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD), a design system/style guide, and all assets (icons, images) ready for development.

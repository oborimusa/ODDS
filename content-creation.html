Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure content matches my brand voice?

We start with a deep discovery session to understand your brand personality, tone, and audience. We also provide samples for your approval before scaling up.

How much does content creation cost?

Pricing depends on content type and volume. Blog posts start from ₦25,000, social media packages from ₦50,000/month. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you handle content strategy or just writing?

Both! We can develop a full content strategy (topics, formats, calendar) or simply execute based on your brief.

How long does it take to create content?

Blog posts typically take 3-5 days, social media packages are delivered weekly, and larger projects have custom timelines.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. We want you to be 100% satisfied. Each project includes up to 2 rounds of revisions.

Can you help with content for specific industries?

Absolutely. Our writers have experience across finance, fashion, tech, hospitality, real estate, and more.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many posts will you create per week?

Our standard package includes 4-5 posts per week per platform, plus daily stories. We can customize based on your needs and budget.

How much does social media management cost?

Packages start from ₦150,000/month for one platform, ₦250,000/month for two platforms, and ₦350,000/month for three platforms. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do I need to provide content?

We can work with your existing content or create everything from scratch — graphics, videos, captions. We just need your input on key messages and promotions.

How soon will I see results?

You'll see engagement increases within the first month. Significant follower growth typically takes 3-6 months of consistent, strategic posting.

Will I have approval over posts?

Yes. We send a monthly content calendar for your approval before posting. You're always in control of your brand voice.

Do you handle social media ads?

Yes. We can manage paid social campaigns as an add-on service. We'll create the ads, target the right audiences, and optimize for results.

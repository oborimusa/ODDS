FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the brand identity process take?

A complete brand identity package typically takes 3-4 weeks. This includes discovery, strategy, design, and revisions.

How much does a brand identity cost?

Pricing depends on complexity and scope. We offer packages starting from ₦250,000 for SMEs. Contact us for a custom quote.

What if I already have a logo but need a full brand identity?

Perfect! We can work with your existing logo and build a complete brand system around it — colors, fonts, guidelines, and more.

What files will I receive?

You'll receive all master files (AI, EPS, PDF) and web-ready formats (PNG, JPG, SVG) for print, digital, and social media.

How many logo concepts do you provide?

We typically present 3 distinct logo concepts for you to choose from, then refine your favorite through 2 rounds of revisions.

Do you also design packaging, signage, or marketing materials?

Yes! Once your brand identity is established, we can apply it to any materials you need — from menus to billboards to social media templates.

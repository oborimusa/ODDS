FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. A simple business site can be ready in 2 weeks, while e-commerce sites may take 3-4 weeks.

Do I need to provide content?

We can work with your existing content or help create new copy and images. We also offer professional copywriting and photography services.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely! All our websites are mobile-first, meaning they're designed specifically for phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Yes! We build on easy-to-use content management systems. We also provide training so you can make updates confidently.

What about hosting and domain?

We can help you register a domain and set up reliable hosting. We offer managed hosting packages with maintenance included.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing varies based on your needs. We offer affordable packages starting from ₦250,000 for SMEs. Contact us for a custom quote.

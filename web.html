FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I do brand design before web design?

Ideally, yes. Your brand identity (logo, colors, fonts) should be established before we build your website. That's why our integrated approach works so well — we do both in the right order.

What if I already have a logo?

Perfect! We can work with your existing brand assets or refresh them as part of the process. We'll build your website around your established identity.

How long does the full process take?

A complete brand + website project typically takes 4-6 weeks. Brand strategy and design (2-3 weeks) followed by web design and development (2-3 weeks).

How much does brand + web design cost?

Combined packages start from ₦450,000, representing better value than hiring separate designers. Contact us for a custom quote based on your needs.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We build on easy-to-use content management systems. We'll train you so you can make updates confidently.

Do you provide brand guidelines?

Absolutely. You'll receive a complete brand guide showing how to use your logo, colors, typography, and visual elements correctly across all media.

How It Started

Our Story

At ODDS, we started with a vision: to empower Nigerian SMEs through premium design and technology. What began as a one-person operation in Osogbo has grown into a full-service digital agency serving businesses across the nation.

"We believe every Nigerian business deserves world-class digital solutions — without the enterprise price tag."

Our founder, Dahiru Obori, started ODDS in 2020 with a laptop, a passion for coding, and an unwavering belief in the potential of local businesses. The name "ODDS" represents our commitment to overcoming challenges — we help our clients beat the odds and thrive in the digital age.

Over the years, our team has grown, our solutions have evolved, and our passion has only intensified. From building websites for startups to transforming brands for regional impact, our journey has been fueled by innovation, resilience, and a genuine love for what we do.

Today, we're proud to be the trusted digital partner for over 50 SMEs across Nigeria, with offices in Lagos and Osogbo. But we're just getting started.

The Path

Our Journey Through Time

2020

The Beginning

ODDS was founded in Osogbo with a mission to help local businesses go digital. Started as a one-person operation serving the first 5 clients.

2021

First Team Member

Hired our first employee — a junior developer. Team expanded to 3, completed 20+ projects in the year.

2022

Expansion to Lagos

Opened Lagos office to better serve our growing client base. Team grew to 6 specialists across design, development, and marketing.

2023

30+ SMEs Served

Celebrated our 30th SME partner milestone. Launched our first e-commerce platform for a major retail client.

2024

50+ Clients & Awards

Reached 50+ SME partners. Founder Dahiru Obori recognized as top tech entrepreneur in Osun State.

2025

Premium Services Launch

Introduced premium service tier with dedicated account managers. Team now 10+ strong with plans for further expansion.

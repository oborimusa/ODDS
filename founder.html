Meet the Founder

Dahiru Obori — Visionary, Full-Stack Developer, Communicator

About Dahiru Obori

Dahiru Obori is a versatile full-stack developer and entrepreneur with deep expertise in multiple industries including hospitality, media, and IT. As an economist and certified digital marketing professional, he has worked closely with marketing and business managers to meet strategic goals and exceed expectations.

As a full-stack developer, Dahiru is highly skilled in:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Git & GitHub Version Control
  • Python & Django Framework
  • Database Management (SQL, Firebase)

He is also an outstanding communicator and speaker, with proven ability to explain complex technical concepts to diverse audiences. From one-on-one sessions to keynotes before hundreds, Dahiru bridges the gap between technology and people.

In addition to his work at ODDS, Dahiru has made his mark in politics, the music scene, and Nollywood — collaborating with politicians, music artists, and film industry influencers across multiple states.

As a visionary founder, he has launched several successful ventures:

  • Drecot Consults – Hospitality recruitment & consulting
  • Shopsafe Delivery Service
  • Hotel Bookings NG
  • First Credmart – An e-commerce platform

He was also one of the early awardees of the Youth Enterprise with Innovation in Nigeria (YouWin!) program. Currently, he is developing a new global platform designed to unite students from across the world.

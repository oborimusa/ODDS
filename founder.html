The Founder's Story

About Dahiru Obori

"I believe technology should empower people, not intimidate them. That's why I bridge the gap between complex code and real-world solutions."

Dahiru Obori is a versatile full-stack developer, graphic designer, and entrepreneur with deep expertise across multiple industries, including hospitality, media, and information technology.

Trained as an economist and certified in digital marketing, virtual assistance, and human resources consulting, he has worked closely with marketing and business managers to achieve strategic goals and consistently exceed expectations.

Technical Expertise

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Bootstrap Git & GitHub Python Django SQL Firebase Graphic Design

He is also an outstanding communicator and speaker, with a proven ability to explain complex technical concepts to diverse audiences. From one-on-one coaching sessions to keynote addresses before hundreds, Dahiru bridges the gap between technology and people.

Beyond his work at ODDS, Dahiru has made his mark in politics, the music scene, and Nollywood — collaborating with politicians, music artists, and film industry influencers across multiple states in Nigeria.

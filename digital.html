FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I see results?

SEO takes 3-6 months to show significant results. Paid ads (Google/Facebook) can drive traffic and leads within 24-48 hours of launch.

How much should I budget for ads?

We recommend starting with ₦100,000-₦200,000 per month for Google/Facebook ads to gather meaningful data. We optimize your budget for maximum ROI.

Do you guarantee top Google rankings?

No ethical agency can guarantee #1 rankings. We guarantee systematic, data-driven work that improves your rankings and traffic over time.

What metrics do you track?

We track traffic, keyword rankings, conversion rates, cost per lead, ROI, and engagement. You'll get monthly reports with all metrics.

Do I need a website for digital marketing?

Yes, a website is your digital storefront. If you don't have one, we can design and build one as part of your marketing package.

Which social media platforms should I use?

It depends on your audience. We'll analyze your target customers and recommend the platforms where they're most active.

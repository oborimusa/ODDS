The Path

Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to empowering SMEs across Nigeria

2020

The Beginning

OneStack was founded in Osogbo with a mission to help local businesses go digital. Started as a one-person operation serving the first 5 clients.

2021

First Team Member

Hired our first employee — a junior developer. Team expanded to 3, completed 20+ projects in the year.

2022

Expansion to Lagos

Opened Lagos operations to better serve our growing client base. Team grew to 5 specialists across design, development, and marketing.

2023

30+ SMEs Served

Celebrated our 30th SME partner milestone. Launched our first e-commerce platform for a major retail client.

2024

50+ Clients & AI Integration

Reached 50+ SME partners. Integrated AI automation and chatbots into our core service offering.

2025

Complete Digital Ecosystem

Launched full-service digital ecosystem — web, SEO, ads, AI, cybersecurity, and training all under one roof. Team now 6+ strong.

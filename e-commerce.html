FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build an online store?

A basic store can be ready in 2-3 weeks. More complex stores with custom features take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a timeline during discovery.

How much does an e-commerce website cost?

Pricing depends on complexity and features. We offer affordable packages starting from ₦350,000 for SMEs. Contact us for a custom quote.

What payment methods can I accept?

All major Nigerian payment gateways: PayStack, Flutterwave, bank transfers, USSD, cards. International payments via PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.

Can I manage my store myself?

Yes! We build easy-to-use admin dashboards where you can add products, process orders, and manage inventory without technical help.

Do you help with product photography?

We can recommend professional photographers or help you optimize existing product images for the web.

What about shipping and logistics?

We integrate with shipping providers (GIG, DHL, CourierPlus) to calculate rates and provide tracking numbers automatically.

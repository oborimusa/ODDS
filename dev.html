Success Story

Custom Solution in Action

Inventory Management System

Retail · Lagos

A growing retail chain with 5 locations was struggling with stock tracking, manual reordering, and data silos. We built a custom inventory management system that:

  • Centralized inventory data across all locations in real-time
  • Automated reordering when stock hits threshold levels
  • Generated sales reports by location, category, and time period
  • Integrated with their existing POS systems

📈 Result: 40% reduction in stockouts, 25% lower inventory holding costs

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom development take?

Timeline depends on complexity. A simple web application can take 4-6 weeks, while complex platforms may take 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during discovery.

How much does custom software cost?

Every project is unique. We provide transparent, fixed-price quotes based on your requirements. Custom software is an investment that pays for itself through efficiency gains.

Do you provide maintenance after launch?

Absolutely! We offer ongoing maintenance packages including updates, security patches, backups, and technical support.

Can you integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. We build APIs and integrations with popular tools like accounting software, CRMs, payment gateways, and more.

Who owns the code and intellectual property?

You do. Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all code, designs, and intellectual property.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage the software?

No. We build user-friendly interfaces that your team can use without technical training. We also provide documentation and training sessions.

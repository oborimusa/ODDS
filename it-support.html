FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need website maintenance?

Yes! Just like a car needs regular oil changes, your website needs regular updates and checks to stay secure, fast, and reliable. Without maintenance, you risk hacks, downtime, and lost customers.

What happens if my site goes down at 3 AM?

Our monitoring system alerts us immediately. We'll start working on a fix right away — even in the middle of the night.

Can you fix a hacked site?

Yes. And if you're on one of our maintenance plans, we'll restore from a clean backup and secure your site to prevent it from happening again.

Do you work with any platform?

We support WordPress, Shopify, WooCommerce, custom sites, and most major platforms. Contact us to confirm compatibility.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time as your needs change.

What's not included in maintenance?

Major design changes, new features, or content creation are not included. But we'd be happy to quote those separately.

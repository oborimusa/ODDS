Local Plumbing Business

Home Services · Lagos

A Lagos-based plumbing company was getting zero leads from their website. They were invisible on Google. We implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy:

  • Keyword research focused on "plumber in Lagos," "emergency plumber," and related terms
  • Optimized all pages with targeted keywords and improved meta descriptions

  • Built local citations across Nigerian directories
  • Created service-specific content pages for different plumbing services
  • Earned backlinks from local business associations

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You'll typically see initial improvements in 3-6 months, with significant results in 6-12 months. It's an investment in sustainable growth.

How much does SEO cost?

Monthly SEO packages start from ₦200,000. Pricing depends on your industry competitiveness, current site state, and goals. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings?

No ethical SEO agency can guarantee #1 rankings. We guarantee strategic, white-hat work that improves your rankings and traffic over time.

What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?

SEO earns free traffic over time. Google Ads gets you immediate traffic but costs per click. Both have their place — we can advise on the best mix for your business.

Do you work with local businesses only?

We work with both local and national businesses. Our local SEO expertise helps you dominate your city, while our broader SEO strategies can target national audiences.

Will I receive regular reports?

Yes. You'll receive monthly reports showing keyword rankings, traffic, conversions, and the work we've done.

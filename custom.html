FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom development take?

Timeline depends on complexity. A simple web application can take 4-6 weeks, while complex platforms may take 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during discovery.

How much does custom software cost?

Every project is unique. We provide transparent, fixed-price quotes based on your requirements. Custom software is an investment that pays for itself through efficiency gains and competitive advantage.

Do you provide maintenance after launch?

Absolutely! We offer ongoing maintenance packages including updates, security patches, backups, technical support, and feature additions.

Can you integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. We build custom APIs and integrations with popular tools like accounting software (QuickBooks), CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), payment gateways (PayStack, Flutterwave), and more.

Who owns the code and intellectual property?

You do. Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all code, designs, database schemas, and intellectual property. No hidden licensing.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage the software?

No. We build user-friendly interfaces that your team can use without technical training. We also provide comprehensive documentation and training sessions.

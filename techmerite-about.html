About TechMerite Academy

TechMerite Academy is a leading technology training institution in Africa, empowering individuals and organisations with cutting-edge digital skills for the continent’s evolving tech landscape.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital skills gap across Africa by providing accessible, world-class training that equips learners and businesses to thrive in the modern economy.

Our Vision

A continent where every community and organisation harnesses technology to create opportunity, innovation, and sustainable growth.

Our Approach

From free monthly community programmes to enterprise-level training, we blend practical, hands-on learning with mentorship and career support.

Leadership

Our team of educators, engineers, and entrepreneurs brings years of experience in global tech companies, ensuring every course stays relevant and forward-looking.

Impact

Thousands of learners across multiple African countries have already gained new skills and advanced their careers through TechMerite’s bootcamps and corporate programmes.

Join Our Mission

Whether you’re an individual eager to learn, a company seeking workforce training, or a partner who shares our vision, we invite you to collaborate with TechMerite Academy.

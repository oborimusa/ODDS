Meet the Founder

Obori Dahiru — Economist, Tech Leader, Visionary

About Obori Dahiru

Obori Dahiru boasts a wealth of knowledge spanning various industries, including hospitality, media, and IT. As an economist and an expert in digital marketing and social media management, he has closely collaborated with marketing and business managers, consistently meeting deadlines and achieving business objectives.

Going beyond his technical prowess, Obori Dahiru is a distinguished communicator and speaker. He extends his expertise to diverse arenas, including politics, the music industry, and the film industry, working with renowned politicians, music stars, and Nollywood stars. His advocacy spans states, championing the ideals of his clients.

As a notable founder and co-founder, Obori established Drecot Consults, specializing in recruitment, hotel management, and consulting within the hospitality sector. Additionally, he founded Shopsafe Delivery Service, Hotel Bookings NG, and First Credmart, an e-commerce company.

His commitment to innovation earned him recognition as one of the pioneering awardees of Youth Enterprise with Innovation in Nigeria (YouWin!). Currently, Obori is working on a global platform to connect students worldwide.

Obori’s exceptional communication skills shine as he effortlessly explains intricate technical concepts to diverse audiences — from one-on-one coaching to addressing gatherings of over 600 people.
